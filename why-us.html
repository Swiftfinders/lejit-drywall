The Lejit Drywall Quality Difference

Many contractors can hang drywall. Far fewer can deliver the flawless finish that transforms a space. Here's what makes our work exceptional:

Superior Materials

We use only premium-grade drywall, tape, mud, and fasteners. Quality materials are the foundation of quality results, and we never cut corners to save a few dollars.

Master-Level Finishing

Our finishers have decades of combined experience creating perfectly smooth, seamless surfaces. Multiple coats applied with precision, sanded to perfection, and inspected rigorously.

Attention to Detail

From the first measurement to the final inspection, we obsess over details. Square corners, invisible seams, and smooth surfaces that look perfect from every angle.

Clean Worksite

Drywall work creates dust, but we minimize mess with proper containment, daily cleanup, and thorough final cleaning. We leave your space cleaner than we found it.
