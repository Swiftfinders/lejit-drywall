Mudding & Taping

The difference between amateur and professional drywall work comes down to mudding and taping. Our expert finishers create perfectly smooth, seamless surfaces ready for painting.

Our Process:

  • First coat: Embed paper tape in compound
  • Second coat: Fill and smooth with precision
  • Third coat: Final finish for seamless surface
  • Sanding between coats for perfection
  • Corner work with attention to detail
  • Fastener concealment for invisible seams

Finish Levels:

  • Level 3: Standard for textured surfaces
  • Level 4: Smooth finish for flat paint
  • Level 5: Premium glass-smooth finish

All joints are invisible when complete, with no ridges, bumps, or tool marks.

Patch & Repair

From small holes to extensive damage, we restore your walls and ceilings to perfect condition. Our repair work is virtually undetectable, blending seamlessly with existing surfaces.

Common Repairs:

  • Nail pops and screw pops
  • Small to large holes (door handles, accidents)
  • Cracks in walls and ceilings
  • Water damage restoration
  • Textured surface matching
  • Corner damage repair

Our Approach:

  • Assess underlying cause of damage
  • Remove damaged material completely
  • Reinforce and rebuild structure
  • Match existing texture perfectly
  • Prime repaired areas for painting

Popcorn Ceiling Removal

Modernize your home with smooth, contemporary ceilings. We safely remove dated popcorn texture and create beautiful smooth finishes that increase property value.

Our Process:
